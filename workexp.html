Work Experience

In my professional journey, I've had the privilege of gaining valuable experience in the tech industry, where I've had the opportunity to put my knowledge and skills into practice. One significant role I held was as an Associate Software Engineer at Synamedia in Chennai, India. During my time there, I was involved in developing enterprise software solutions, focusing particularly on the eRDK (Enterprise Reference Design Kit) project. In this role, I worked on tasks such as collecting Telemetry Data from Customer Premises Equipment (CPE) devices and sending it to the Cloud. I gained hands-on experience with C++, Linux-based systems, and embedded software, all while operating in an Agile development environment.

One of the highlights of my experience at Synamedia was my involvement in refining device configuration management using Xconf and working on both RDK-Broadband and RDK-Video components. I collaborated closely with a team of talented individuals, contributing to the manual testing of Comcast CBRv2 routers and developing new features. Through these experiences, I not only enhanced my technical skills but also learned the importance of effective teamwork, problem-solving, and adaptability in a fast-paced industry.

Currently, I am continuing to expand my horizons as a Master Thesis Student at Ericsson in Luleå, Sweden. Here, I am engaged in groundbreaking research focused on climate control optimization in radio base stations using AI and machine learning techniques. This role allows me to apply my academic knowledge to real-world challenges while contributing to the advancement of technology in a meaningful way. Additionally, I am honored to serve as a Student Ambassador at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, where I actively support and engage with the academic community and the upcoming students.
